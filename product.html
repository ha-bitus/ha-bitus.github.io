Our Mission

At Habitus, we believe in reward-reinforced habit building. Our mission is to help people maintain healthy habits by rewarding them for going to the gym.

How it works

With the Habitus app, you will receive a weekly reward for going to the gym at least 3 times a week. By scanning a QR code at your chosen gym at least three times, you will receive a bar code which will allow you to get a free protein bar supplied by us. This reward will be redeemable in one of our partner gyms.
